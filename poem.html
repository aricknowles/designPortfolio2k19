Self Esteem

A site made as a reaction to Nayyirah Waheed's "the color of low self esteem". As the poem itself is short but impactful, the site expands this experience, breaking each line into a single page. The loop of ocean waves in the background juxtaposes with the disorienting and uncomfortable whispers. This emphasizes the bittersweet tone of the poem, that speaks to the complex feelings that comes from knowledge that can only be gained from experience.→
