Aviva Frank, Sociologist

The website was designed for sociologist Aviva Frank, who required a site that would be used to apply to graduate school, as well as showcase previous and upcoming talks. The client requested that the website be easy to use, minimal and clean. The client also requested that the website match her business cards that adorned a blue and green brush stroke. Most importantly, the client requested that the website be easy enough to update information on that she could do so when needed.→
