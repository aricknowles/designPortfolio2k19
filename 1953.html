1953

1953 is an interactive VR experience that explores the structures and history that molded Iran into the country that we know it as today. Based off of the events that led to the former Iranian Prime Minister, Mohammad Mosaddegh, to be put under house arrest, the viewer has the opportunity to experience the house that he spent his last years in. Inside, the viewer is able to uncover different pieces of the history found in the news clips, newspapers, and direct quotes. The goal for this project is to leave the viewer questioning how small actions can create bigger impacts.

1953 was made in Cinema 4d and Unity.
