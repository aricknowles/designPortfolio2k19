a woman

a woman was created as a piece in collaboration with the poet Judith Sloan, the designer Emily Bottner, and the White Plains BID (Business Improvement District). The goal of the collaboration was to bring life to vacant storefronts in the city of White Plains through art. Judith Sloan wrote the poem, “a woman” as a way to acknowledge, embrace and celebrate women in the wake of the 2016 election. Working in collaboration with Emily Bottner, a 3d model in Cinema 4D was designed after a paper mockup to allow for better control as well as a higher resolution image.

Poem by Judith Sloan. Typeset by Emily Bottner. 3D modeling and final image by Aric Knowles.
