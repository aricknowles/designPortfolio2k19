Amy’s Safe Bake

A set of cookie tins for an organic and vegan cookie company, Amy’s Safe Bake. The goal of the design was to create something friendly and fun, but not overwhelming. The cans have a distinct front and back, with a pattern that wraps around to unify both. The lid shows the same cookie found on the front of the can. The copy was provided by the client, who also specified the desire for paint cans.
