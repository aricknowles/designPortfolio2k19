Prospect Mills

A concept bottle for a vodka by Prospect Mills, a distillery in Shasta county California. The objective was to make a bottle that would speak to the distilleries “old western” themes with a modern twist. The intricacies of the twisting vines intermingling with the type were an ode to the ornate framings found on old western posters, but exploded around the bottle to better appreciate their quality. The bottle was designed in Illustrator and printed on vinyl which was then hand placed with care.
