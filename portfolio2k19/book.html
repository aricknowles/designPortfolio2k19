Typography

"Typography" is a book that outlines the different typographic terms, in a visual and easy to read format. Learning typographic terms can sometimes be overwhelming on those that may not feel inclined towards it. The book was developed to help make the process more engaging, and nudge the viewer into understanding why learning the terms in the first place isn’t just meaningless jargon. The book is 74 pages of color and fun, that is heavy in its use of illustration as a teaching method.
